### The "Visibility Layer" Thesis (NEW Framework)
Across every opportunity, the winning pattern is making **invisible costs visible**:
- N22T on power — but most businesses don't know their cost/kWh
- N1.62T in FX losses — but no accessible exposure tracking
- N1.2T in demurrage — but no clearing agent accountability data
- 62 taxes — but no deadline/compliance tracking

**This is a platform thesis:** Sell visibility tools first (low capital, fast to build), then layer optimization and financing on top.

### Solar ROI Wedge Model (NEW)
Don't sell solar panels (capital-intensive). Sell the dashboard that makes the CASE for solar. Then earn referral fees from solar installers. Customer pays for visibility; solar companies pay for qualified leads.

### Data-as-Product for Agriculture (NEW)
Instead of building cold chain infrastructure ($$$), build a loss data platform and sell subscriptions:
- Government agencies: $10-20K/month
- Cold chain investors: $5-10K/month
- Agricultural processors: $5K/month
- Insurance companies: $5-10K/month

### Pattern Analysis for Winning Products
1. Visibility tools beat capital-intensive solutions
2. Compliance/risk reduction sells (penalty avoidance = clear ROI)
3. Tools that help CFOs justify bigger purchases to boards
4. Integration over reinvention — plug into existing systems
