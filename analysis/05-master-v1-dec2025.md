## The Core Investment Thesis (One Line)
Nigerian businesses are already spending $43-68B/year on broken workarounds. Build something 20%+ better, price it below their current spend, and you capture redirected budget — not new budget.

## Key Risk Factors
- FX volatility: 40.9% naira depreciation in one year — price in USD or hedge
- Default rates: 15-25% without proper underwriting
- 2025 funding selectivity: only 27 of 500+ fintechs funded
- Multinational exodus signals environment getting harder
- Collections reality: clients often pay 6+ months late
