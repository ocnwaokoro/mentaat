## 3. What the New Ideas Change

### VendorScore cracks the top 5

At 7.20, VendorScore ranks #4 — above TaxGuard and CashFlow AI. Why:
- **Fintech upside (9):** Credit scoring data is the foundation for lending, insurance, and trade finance. This is infrastructure.
- **Market size (8):** Every B2B transaction involves credit risk. The 13% credit bureau coverage gap is a N49.4T+ opportunity.
- **Competition (8):** No Nigerian B2B credit scoring tool exists.
- **It's a PLATFORM play:** VendorScore data makes PayMatch smarter (score customers by payment history), SupplyLink viable (score invoice buyers), and CollectPro more effective (prioritize by debtor risk).

### BuildTrack is surprisingly strong

At 7.00, tied with CollectPro. Construction has the highest expansion outlook (80.8 index) of ANY sector in the CBN data. Zero Nigerian construction software exists. Material price volatility (cement 150%+ increase) creates daily pain.

### InsureMatch has the largest untouched market

0.34% insurance penetration with 56% premium growth. N1.56T market. Zero cost to acquire customers (commission model). But Skill Fit (5) and Time to Revenue (5) drag it down — this is a business development play, not an engineering play.

### WaterTrack is NOT standalone

Score 5.05 confirms what the gaps doc said: build this as a feature inside GenTrack, not a separate product.

### ExitBridge is time-limited

Score 5.60 reflects the reality: this is a window-dependent opportunity that may close as multinationals finish exiting and talent emigrates. Not worth building unless you've already got revenue from a primary product.

## 4. Updated Portfolio Strategy

### Phase 1 (Month 1-2): PayMatch
- Invoice-to-payment matching SaaS
- Target Food/Bev distributors first
- Score: 8.25 — highest of all 17 products
- Revenue target: 10 paying customers, $5-10K MRR

### Phase 2 (Month 3-4): ForexGuard
- FX exposure dashboard
- Upsell to PayMatch customers who are importers
- Cognitive bias data = unique sales hook
- Revenue target: 10 customers at N150-250K/month

### Phase 3 (Month 4-6): ClearFast + VendorScore (in parallel)
- ClearFast: customs AI for clearing agents (different buyer = no cannibalization)
- VendorScore: credit scoring (feeds data to PayMatch + future SupplyLink)
- Both leverage AI/ML skills; ClearFast uses OCR, VendorScore uses credit modeling

### Phase 4 (Month 6-8): CashFlow AI
- Working capital forecasting with lender referrals
- Uses PayMatch invoice data + VendorScore credit data
- CP recommendation module (18% vs 35.5%)
- Lender referral commissions as second revenue stream

### Phase 5 (Month 8-12): Platform convergence
- PayMatch + ForexGuard + CashFlow AI + VendorScore = Nigerian CFO toolkit
- ClearFast = import chain product (add maritime upstream)
- TaxGuard/BuildTrack/InsureMatch = expansion candidates based on traction

### The End State
A Nigerian B2B financial intelligence platform with 5+ products sharing:
- Proprietary invoice, payment, and credit data
- Common auth/billing/notification infrastructure
- USSD/SMS fallback on all products
- "Expand without hiring" positioning across the board
- Food/Beverage sector as the primary beachhead
